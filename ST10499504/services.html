Our Services

From the moment you book to the moment you check out, we've got you covered. Here's everything included when you stay with Swift Bookings.

Room Types

Standard Room

Our Standard Room is designed for guests who want comfort without the extras. You get a queen-size bed, flat-screen TV, free high-speed Wi-Fi, a work desk, air conditioning, and a private bathroom with shower. Everything you need for a solid night's rest.

  • Queen bed
  • En-suite bathroom
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Flat-screen TV
  • Air conditioning
  • Daily housekeeping

Rate: From R850 per night

Capacity: Up to 2 guests

Deluxe Suite

The Deluxe Suite is for guests who want space and a bit more luxury. It includes a king-size bed, a separate lounge area, a minibar, premium bath products, and a private balcony with a view. If you're celebrating something or just treating yourself, this is the one.

  • King bed
  • Separate lounge area
  • Private balcony
  • Minibar (stocked on request)
  • Premium toiletries
  • Free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TV
  • Air conditioning
  • Daily housekeeping and turndown service

Rate: From R1 950 per night

Capacity: Up to 2 guests

Included With Every Stay

Express Online Check-in

No need to wait at the front desk. Complete your check-in online up to 24 hours before arrival. We'll have your key card ready when you walk in.

Complimentary Breakfast

A full breakfast is included with every booking. We serve hot and cold options daily from 06:30 to 10:00 in the dining room. There's always something for everyone, including vegetarian options.

24/7 Guest Support

Our front desk is staffed around the clock. Whether it's a late-night request or an early morning need, someone is always available to help.

Additional Services

These services are available on request and may carry additional charges. You can enquire about them when you make your booking.

Airport Shuttle Service

We offer a private shuttle to and from King Shaka International Airport and OR Tambo International Airport. Shuttles can be booked for arrival, departure, or both. Just provide your flight details at least 24 hours before you need the transfer.

  • Private vehicle (not shared with other guests)
  • Professional, licensed driver
  • Available 24 hours a day
  • Child seat available on request

Rate: From R350 per trip (one way)

  • Swedish and deep tissue massage
  • Facial treatments
  • Body scrubs and wraps
  • Couples treatments available
  • Sauna and steam room access

Treatments from: R450 per session

Operating hours: 08:00 – 20:00 daily
